Eddyline Ventures

Context & Brief

Eddyline Ventures is a strategic business consultancy. I was hired alongside a computer science student, to lead the redesign and redevelopment of its website during a month placement, to bring a more modern aesthetic and increase the website’s leads via an efficient CTA.

Approach

In week one, I carried out secondary research to understand my audience. I created three personas to inform the site's information architecture and user flow. In week two, I prototyped four pages (Home, About, Mission and Contact) for three different media (phone, tablet and laptop) on Figma. Finally, I developed the website using HTML, CSS and JavaScript over the last two weeks.

Results

The new website was delivered on time and we received very positive feedback from the client. View the final result here.

Project Retrospective

Looking back, I think one thing I could have done differently would have been to develop the site using a CMS like wordpress or webflow. I've since taken a course to learn webflow and I think it would have allowed for a smoother transition and handover to my client.
