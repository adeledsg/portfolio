Be Wild

About

I developed Be Wild with the purpose of generating an engaging reading experience for tweens.

Recognising Interactive Digital Storytelling (IDS) as an experimental design space, I first conducted secondary research to understand the interests of my target audience. I then constructed a narrative, created graphical elements, developed scrolling interactions, and deployed the story.

Be Wild’s story follows the adventures of a young wildebeest on his first migration. Its narrative employs the classic structure of the hero’s journey, enriched with anthropomorphic characteristic. Ultimately, Be Wild is a proposition for an engaging reading experience for tweens that aims to illustrate a different avenue in the field of IDS to engage tweens in stories.
