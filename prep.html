Prep

About

I designed Prep, a mobile application that generates an effective learning experience for students. To answer essential learning and motivational needs uncovered during user research, I designed interactions and features inspired by the Pomodoro technique that help students achieve deep focus and put in place effective study habits.

Personas & user flows

I carried out user research via questionnaires, sent to 81 participants, which informed my creation of user personas. I defined my users’ study habits and motivational needs via quantitative and qualitative questions. The personas developed then guided the design of interactions. For an effective learning experience, the navigation to the workflow is simple yet meets diverse needs. Prep offers three distinct methods for creating workflows. I also introduced a calendar and forum page, recognising teamwork and impending deadlines as key motivators from the research findings.

View the research results, personas and user flows here. The study was carried with French students.
